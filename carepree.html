Carepree

A Case Study

Project Overview

Carepree is a mobile application that enables patients to procure online consultations from doctors. The application is designed to provide a seamless experience for patients and healthcare providers. It has a user-friendly interface that allows patients to view doctors’ profiles, availability, and book appointments at their convenience.

Problem Statement

Timely and appropriate healthcare assistance is a challenge for many. Some patients may live in areas with a shortage of specialists, making it difficult for them to get the care they need. Some hospitals may be overbooked, leading to long wait times and delays in receiving treatment. Some hospitals may have limited hours of operation, making it difficult for patients to schedule appointments outside of regular business hours. Some hospitals may be overwhelmed with emergencies and may not be able to take appointments.

Solution

Carepree will save patients’ time by providing user-friendly interface to book and schedule appointments. It will have access to many specialists, pertaining to a patient’s requirement. It will provide easy and 24/7 consultation.
